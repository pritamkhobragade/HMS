Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

Our hospital is open 24/7 for emergency services. Regular outpatient services are available Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

You can schedule an appointment through our online booking system, by calling our reception at (123) 456-7891, or by visiting our hospital in person. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

Insurance & Billing

We accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our billing department at (123) 456-7892 to verify your insurance coverage and benefits.

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, cash, and online payments through our secure patient portal. Payment plans are also available for eligible patients.

Patient Care

Please bring your ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results. For new patients, please arrive 15 minutes early to complete registration forms.

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal. To register, please visit our reception desk or contact our medical records department at (123) 456-7893.

COVID-19 Information

We maintain strict safety protocols including mandatory mask-wearing, social distancing, regular sanitization, and temperature checks. All staff are fully vaccinated and follow CDC guidelines.

COVID-19 testing is available at our drive-through testing center. Appointments are required and can be scheduled through our online booking system or by calling (123) 456-7894.
